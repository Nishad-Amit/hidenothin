05

SECTION / TRANSPARENCY

How we work

ENGAGEMENT
4 to 12 weeks, start to ship.
Most projects land between six and eight. We tell you the realistic number on the call.
PRICING
We talk it through on the first call.
Scoped to the work, quoted before we start. No hidden line items, no surprise invoices.
WHO WORKS ON IT
The named team below.
No account managers. No junior hand-off. You speak to the people drawing and writing the code.
OUTSOURCED
Nothing. We do the work.
If a project genuinely needs a specialist we trust, we say so and introduce you directly.
COMMUNICATION
One shared doc. One weekly call.
No decks. The doc is yours to keep after the project ends.
